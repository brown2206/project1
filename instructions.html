POWER TOWER OF HANOI

INSTRUCTIONS

  • The objective of POWER TOWER OF HANOI is to move the entire stack of weights to the 3rd stack.
  • Only the top weight of each stack can be moved at a time.
  • In this game, a successful SELECTION and DROP consists of a 2-click cycle.
    • The 1st click of the maroon colored base of a stack selects the weight that sits ATOP of that stack.
    • The 2nd click of a maroon colored base of a stack moves your selected weight to the very TOP of that stack.
    • As a user, you will continue to SELECT and DROP weights using this 2-click process.
  • If the 1st weight that you've SELECTED is lighter than the weight that you'd like to DROP on, then your weight drop will be successful.
  • If the first weight that you've SELECTED is heavier than the weight that you'd like to DROP on, then your move will be rejected.
